## What is a Chief Mogul?

A Chief Mogul or CM is more than just a club administrator; you are the founder, leader, and primary facilitator of your mogul Club community. You set the tone, define the focus, guide discussions, and play a crucial role in fostering an environment where members can learn, connect, and invest together.
